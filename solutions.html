Our Solutions

Aerial Surveying and Mapping

Utilize drones equipped with high-resolution cameras or LiDAR sensors to capture aerial imagery or collect 3D point cloud data. Process the collected data using GIS software to create accurate and up-to-date maps, elevation models, and orthophotos.

Asset Inspection and Monitoring

Conduct aerial inspections of infrastructure assets such as buildings, bridges, power lines, and pipelines using drones. Use GIS analysis to assess asset condition, detect anomalies, and monitor changes over time, enabling proactive maintenance and risk management.

Precision Agriculture

Offer drone-based solutions for precision agriculture, including crop health assessment, vegetation mapping, and yield estimation. Combine drone imagery with GIS analysis to optimize crop management practices, improve resource allocation, and enhance productivity.
